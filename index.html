Dual Force

Heroes. One World. One Chance. Dual Force is a fast-paced 2D fantasy action-adventure game where two heroes stand together against a world slowly being consumed by darkness. Play solo or in local co-op, and rely on precise combat, quick reflexes, and constant progression to survive. An unknown and destructive force threatens all life. Your mission is to join the Dual Force, grow stronger with every battle, and push back the evil before the world is lost forever. Key Features Fast, arcade-style combat – responsive controls, fluid movement, and intense action Two playable heroes – distinct abilities and complementary combat styles Local co-op gameplay – play together on a shared or split screen Dark fantasy world – dangerous enemies, atmospheric environments, and heroic battles Roguelike elements – progression, new abilities, and fresh challenges every run Full controller support – a smooth, console-like experience on PC Dual Force is built for players who enjoy challenge, tight action gameplay, and cooperative experiences. A world on the brink of collapse awaits—and it will not wait for you.

Penalty

Every Choice Has a Consequence Penalty is a 2D open-world fantasy action RPG where the world does not simply react to your actions — it judges them. Explore a dangerous universe, fight powerful enemies, grow stronger, and shape your own path, knowing that every decision comes at a cost. At its core, Penalty is built around freedom and consequences. Use weapons, magic, and equipment to customize your character while navigating a living, open world where there is rarely a clear line between right and wrong. Key Features 2D open-world RPG – free exploration, hidden areas, and dangerous encounters Action-focused combat system – combine weapons and magic in dynamic battles Deep character progression and customization – armor, gear, and abilities Single-player and multiplayer modes – co-op and PvP support Consequence-driven gameplay – your choices influence the world and how it responds Full controller support – smooth gameplay on both keyboard and controller Penalty is designed for players who value meaningful decisions, deep RPG systems, and true player agency. There are no actions without consequences — only the paths you choose to walk.

ABOUT ME

Every Choice Has a Consequence Howling Beast Entertainment is not a massive team, but rather the passionate workshop of a solo creator. As a solo developer, I have been walking the path of video game development for over a decade – from programming to art, from 2D and 3D animation to illustration, I have learned and built every part of the process on my own. For me, game development is not just work, it is a calling. Every project I create aims to bring to life the worlds born from my imagination and to offer players something truly unique and memorable. Hand-drawn animations, custom combat systems, deep stories, and the freedom to explore are all core elements of what I make. Even though I work alone, my vision is always big: to create games that leave an impression, inspire, and provide unforgettable experiences. This journey has been full of challenges, but each obstacle has only strengthened my belief that creativity and perseverance know no limits. To me, Howling Beast Entertainment is more than just a name – it is a promise to always give my very best, and to deliver works born from passion and honesty to the players.
