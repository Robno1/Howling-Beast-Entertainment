About us

My name is ROB, and I’m a passionate solo indie game developer working under my newly founded studio, Howling Beast Entertainment. For over 11 years, I’ve been dedicated to bringing my dream of creating a truly unique video game to life. Currently, I’m developing a 2D open-world RPG that offers not only a fresh visual style but also original gameplay mechanics never seen before. My goal is to build a world that’s exciting, immersive, and unforgettable—whether you're fighting, exploring, or experiencing the story. Every aspect of the game—from the art and animation to the coding and design—is crafted entirely by me. I'm always open to ideas, suggestions, and feedback that can help make this game an even more special experience for players around the world.
