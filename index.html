Penalty Gameplay

Explore a magical 2D fantasy world where the action never stops! This open-world action RPG comes to life with unique, hand-drawn FBF animation and challenges your reflexes with a dynamic real-time combat system. Fight, grow, explore – the world is yours to conquer!

Create own hero

At the start of your journey, you must choose: will you play as a male or female hero? Your choice affects more than just appearance – each gender has unique strengths. The male character is physically stronger, excelling in melee combat, while the female character is quicker and smarter, offering advantages in magic and traps. Choose wisely – your path depends on it!

Awesome game mechanics

This game breaks the mold with completely custom systems you won’t find anywhere else! Mix and match armor pieces freely to shape both your character’s look and abilities. The unique weapon system lets you discover your own combat style, while the hand-crafted fight mechanics make every battle thrilling and unpredictable. Tactics, style, and freedom – all in your hands.
