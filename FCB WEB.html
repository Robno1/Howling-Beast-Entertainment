FOOTBAL CARD BATTLE

Heroes. One World. One Chance. This game is a unique blend of football and card-based strategy, where two players compete against each other in real time. The goal is simple: score 5 goals before your opponent. But the path to victory is anything but. During the match, you use cards to build tactics, strengthen your players, and gain the upper hand on the field. Every decision counts—if you run out of cards or your players become exhausted and ineffective, you can quickly lose control of the match. Each team consists of 6 players on the field: 5 outfield players and 1 goalkeeper. That’s a total of 12 players competing at the same time. The gameplay is enhanced by special magic cards that: temporarily or permanently boost player stats grant unique abilities provide tactical advantages in critical moments The right strategy, timing, and smart card usage will determine who scores the 5th goal—and wins the match.
